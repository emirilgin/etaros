# Sidekick — Go-To-Market & Positioning

## What It Is (One Line)
**Your AI that watches your screen and protects you in real time.**

## Tagline Options
1. "Your second pair of eyes." ← recommended — personal, clear
2. "Catches what you miss."
3. "Always watching. Always on your side."

## The Problem It Solves
- The average person unknowingly pays **$219/month** in forgotten subscriptions
- AI-generated phishing attacks increased **1,200%** in 2024
- Identity theft affects **1 in 5 adults** annually
- No existing tool watches your *screen* in real time to catch these

## Why Sidekick Wins
| Competitor | Gap Sidekick fills |
|---|---|
| Microsoft Recall | Sends screenshots to cloud — privacy nightmare |
| McAfee / Norton | Only blocks known threats, no real-time screen analysis |
| Cleo / YNAB | Reviews transactions after the fact — too late |
| Rewind AI | General recall, no protection focus |

**Sidekick's moat:** AI vision running locally with your own API key. Nothing leaves your machine.

## Target User
- Age 22–40
- Shops online regularly
- Has been burned by a scam, surprise charge, or auto-renewal
- Values privacy — uncomfortable with apps that "watch" them for a company
- Not a tech nerd. Wants peace of mind, not configuration.

## Focus Modes = Revenue Hooks
| Mode | Tier | Value prop |
|---|---|---|
| Ghost | Free | Privacy & scam guard — the hook |
| Vault | Pro | Financial guardian — saves money |
| Bloom | Pro | Health & food coach |
| Flow | Pro | Focus & productivity guard |
| Hawk | Pro | Deal hunter, price comparison |

## Pricing
- **Free**: 15 messages — enough to get hooked
- **Pro** $9/mo — unlimited, all modes. Pays for itself the first subscription trap it catches.
- **Max** $39/mo — Pro + better AI model + priority features

**Comparison:** McAfee Total Protection = $99/yr. Norton 360 = $109/yr. Sidekick Pro = $108/yr. Same price bracket, 10x more useful.

## Investor Pitch (30 seconds)
> "Sidekick is an AI desktop companion that watches your screen in real time. It catches scams, subscription traps, and bad deals before they cost you — without sending any data to a server. We're building the privacy-first security layer that Microsoft Recall proved there's massive demand for, but failed to deliver on privacy. Pro is $9/month. Pays for itself the first time it catches an auto-renewal."

## App Name
**Sidekick** — keep it. Clean, memorable, positions as a companion (not a tool), works cross-culturally.
