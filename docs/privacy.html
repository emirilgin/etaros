Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · This notice is provided under Articles 13 & 14 of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / AVG).

Etaros ("Etaros", "we", "us") is a security tool that helps you detect scams, phishing, and fraud. Protecting your data is core to what we do. This policy explains what personal data we process, why, on what legal basis, who we share it with, how long we keep it, and the rights you have.

1. Who is responsible (data controller)

Etaros is an independent project run by a private individual based in the Netherlands. The controller responsible for your personal data can be reached at:

  • Project: Etaros
  • Email: etaros.app@gmail.com

We have not appointed a Data Protection Officer, as we are not required to under Article 37 GDPR. For any privacy question, or to receive our full contact details, use the email above.

3. Where your screen data goes

When the app analyses your screen, the relevant image is sent to an AI model to produce a verdict and is then discarded. We do not keep a history of your screen. Analysis providers act as our processors under data-processing terms.

5. International transfers

Some processors above are located outside the European Economic Area (mainly the United States). Where data is transferred there, it is protected by appropriate safeguards under Articles 44-46 GDPR - the EU-US Data Privacy Framework where the provider is certified, and/or the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses. You can request a copy of the relevant safeguards via the contact email.

6. Cookies and local storage

The website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. The live demo stores a small counter in your browser's local storage to enforce the free-trial limit. This is strictly necessary for that feature and stores no personal data. You can clear it any time via your browser.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Rectify inaccurate data;
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten");
  • Restrict or object to processing;
  • Data portability (receive your data in a portable format);
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent;
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority - in the Netherlands, the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens.

To exercise any right, email etaros.app@gmail.com. We respond within one month (Art. 12(3) GDPR).

8. Children

Accounts are intended for users aged 16 or older (the digital-consent age in the Netherlands). If you are under 16, a parent or guardian must consent on your behalf.

9. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and limiting who can access account data. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your data safe and will notify you and the supervisory authority of a breach where legally required.

10. Changes

If this policy changes materially we will update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
