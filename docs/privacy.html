Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Etaros is a security tool built to protect you, so protecting your privacy is the whole point. This explains what we do and don't collect.

What stays on your device

Etaros analyzes your screen to detect scams and phishing. That screen analysis happens through an AI model, but your screenshots are not stored on our servers. They are sent to the AI provider only to produce a result, then discarded. We do not keep a history of what's on your screen.

What we collect

  • Account info: if you create an account, we store your email address (via Supabase) to manage sign-in and your plan. Nothing more.
  • Usage counts: a basic count of analyses, to enforce free/paid limits. Not tied to the content you analyzed.
  • Crash reports: anonymous technical error data (via Sentry) so we can fix bugs.

The website demo

The "try it live" demo on this site sends the text you paste to an AI provider (Groq) for analysis, through a proxy that hides our keys. We do not store what you paste. The waitlist form stores only the email you submit.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data.
  • We never share your email with advertisers.
  • We never store the contents of your screen.

Your rights

You can delete your account and associated data at any time. To request deletion or ask anything privacy-related, email sidekickhelp@gmail.com.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above. Continued use means you accept the current version.
