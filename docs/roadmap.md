# Sidekick — Roadmap to Rich

## Month 1 — Ship & Validate
**Do:**
- Launch beta this week
- Post r/macapps, r/SideProject, Show HN
- TikTok/Instagram showing app catching real scam live
- Gmail for support wired in

**Goal:** 50 downloads, 1-3 paying users, 1 viral post attempt
**Money:** €0-50

---

## Month 2 — Find the hook
**Do:**
- Talk to every user — what do they actually use it for?
- Find the ONE feature that makes people say "whoa"
- Post 5 short demo videos, see which gets views
- Fix top 3 complaints

**Goal:** 200 downloads, €50-200 MRR, know your best angle
**Money:** €50-200/mo

---

## Month 3 — Buy domain + go pro
**Do:**
- Buy `sidekick.app` or best available
- Set up `support@sidekick.app`, real SMTP
- Raise prices if people pay without hesitation
- Find 1 small business to pilot B2B (accountant, advisor, support team)

**Goal:** 500 downloads, €200-500 MRR, 1 B2B pilot
**Money:** €200-500/mo

---

## Month 4 — B2B pivot starts
**Do:**
- Build 1 B2B-specific feature (team dashboard, compliance report, admin panel)
- Charge B2B pilot €200-500/mo (10x consumer price)
- Apply to Dutch startup programs: TechLeap.nl, Startup in Residence, RVO subsidies
- Register KVK if not done → opens grant eligibility

**Goal:** 2-3 B2B clients, €1-2k MRR
**Money:** €1-2k/mo

---

## Month 5 — Product Hunt + press
**Do:**
- Launch on Product Hunt (prep 2 weeks before)
- Reach out to 10 tech journalists with demo video
- Apply for WBSO (Dutch R&D tax credit — up to 32% of dev costs back)
- Build waitlist for enterprise version

**Goal:** 500+ PH upvotes, press mention, 5 B2B leads
**Money:** €2-5k/mo + WBSO refund

---

## Month 6 — Pre-seed raise
**Do:**
- Deck: problem → solution → traction → market → ask
- Traction needed: €3-5k MRR, 20%+ monthly growth, 5+ B2B clients
- Target Dutch VCs: Keen Venture Partners, Volta Ventures, Peak Capital
- Apply EIC Accelerator (EU grant up to €2.5M non-dilutive)
- Ask for €200-500k pre-seed at €2-4M valuation

**Goal:** 2-3 VC meetings, term sheet
**Money:** €3-5k/mo + potential raise

---

## Month 7-12 — Scale with funding
**Do:**
- Hire 1 person (sales or second dev)
- Build enterprise features (SSO, audit logs, team management)
- 10+ B2B clients → case studies → more clients
- Series A pitch prep (need €10-20k MRR)
- Target US market

**Goal:** €10-20k MRR, Series A ready
**Money:** €10-20k/mo

---

## Year 2 — Series A
- €500k-2M raise at €5-15M valuation
- Team of 5-10
- US office or partnerships
- Target acquirers: Microsoft (Defender), Crowdstrike, Palo Alto Networks

---

## Dutch Grants (free money — apply ASAP after KVK)

| Grant | Amount | When |
|---|---|---|
| **WBSO** | 32% dev costs back | After KVK, apply immediately |
| **MIT subsidie** | €20-40k | After KVK |
| **EIC Accelerator** | Up to €2.5M | Month 5-6 |
| **Startup in Residence** | Pilot contracts | Month 3-4 |
| **SIDN Fonds** | €5-25k | Now, digital resilience angle |

WBSO alone = €10-20k back on your own dev time. Apply the month KVK is done.

- **€10k MRR** = fundable in NL
- **€20k MRR** = VCs call you, you don't call them

Pitch: **"Real-time AI compliance monitor that runs on any screen."**

Target markets:
- Financial advisors — compliance violations caught in real time
- Customer support teams — policy breach monitoring
- Call centers — real-time agent coaching
- Insurance companies — fraud detection on claims screens
- Legal firms — risky content flagging

Price: €500-5000/mo per company seat, not €9.
