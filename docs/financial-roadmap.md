# Sidekick — Full Financial Roadmap (Month 1-36)

---

## PHASE 1: SURVIVE (Month 1-3)
*Goal: prove people pay. Spend nothing.*

### Month 1
**Actions:**
- Launch beta (this week)
- Post r/macapps, r/SideProject, Show HN
- 1 demo video showing app catching real scam
- Fix bugs from first users

**Revenue:** €0-50 (maybe 1-3 paying friends)
**Expenses:** €0 (everything free tier)
**Bank:** whatever you have now

---

### Month 2
**Actions:**
- Talk to every single user (DM them)
- Find the ONE feature that makes people say "whoa"
- Post 5 short videos across TikTok/Instagram/Twitter
- Raise prices to €12/mo Pro, €24/mo Max (test willingness)
- Apply SIDN Fonds (no KVK needed, up to €25k)

**Revenue:** €50-200/mo
**Expenses:** €0
**Bank:** +€100-400 total

---

### Month 3
**Actions:**
- KVK registration done → immediately apply WBSO
- Apply MIT subsidie
- Find 1 business to pilot B2B (free or €99/mo)
- Buy domain if 10+ paying users
- Hire nobody, do everything yourself

**Revenue:** €200-500/mo
**Expenses:** €10-50/mo (domain, email)
**Grants incoming:** SIDN decision (€5-25k)
**Bank:** €500-1500 total + grant incoming

---

## PHASE 2: TRACTION (Month 4-6)
*Goal: €3-5k MRR. This unlocks funding.*

### Month 4
**Actions:**
- WBSO approved → submit first dev hours (get 32% back quarterly)
- B2B pilot → charge €200-500/mo
- Build 1 B2B feature: admin dashboard or compliance report
- TechLeap.nl application
- 3 demo videos/week minimum

**Revenue:** €500-1500/mo (mix B2C + B2B)
**WBSO refund:** €2-5k (first quarter dev time back)
**Expenses:** €50-100/mo
**Bank:** €2-5k total

---

### Month 5
**Actions:**
- Product Hunt launch (biggest B2C spike possible)
- Reach out to 10 tech journalists with video demo
- 3-5 B2B pilots → convert 2 to paying (€200-500/mo each)
- Start building waitlist for "Enterprise" tier (€999/mo)
- EIC Accelerator application prep

**Revenue:** €1500-3000/mo
**Expenses:** €100/mo
**Bank:** €5-10k total

---

### Month 6
**Actions:**
- Build pitch deck (problem/solution/traction/market/ask)
- Cold email 20 Dutch angels + VCs: Keen Venture Partners, Volta Ventures, Peak Capital, Newion
- EIC Accelerator submit (non-dilutive, up to €2.5M)
- 5+ B2B clients → case studies → credibility
- Target: close pre-seed or EIC

**Revenue:** €3000-5000/mo
**Expenses:** €100-200/mo
**Bank:** €10-15k total
**Funding target:** €200-500k pre-seed OR €50-150k EIC grant

---

## PHASE 3: RAISE & SCALE (Month 7-12)
*Goal: close funding. Hit €20k MRR.*

### Month 7-8 — Close pre-seed
**If pre-seed closes (€200-500k for 10-15% equity):**

**How to spend it:**
| Expense | Monthly | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Your salary | €3000/mo | Pay yourself first |
| 1 hire (sales/dev) | €3000-4000/mo | You can't do everything |
| Marketing/ads | €1000/mo | Test paid acquisition |
| Infrastructure | €500/mo | Scale servers |
| Legal/accounting | €500/mo | KVK, contracts, VAT |
| **Total burn** | **€8-9k/mo** | |

**Runway:** €200k / €9k burn = ~22 months
**Goal:** reach €20k MRR before runway ends

**Revenue:** €5000-8000/mo
**Net (after burn):** -€3-4k/mo (investing in growth)

---

### Month 9-10 — B2B acceleration
**Actions:**
- 10+ B2B clients at €200-500/mo each = €2-5k MRR from B2B alone
- Enterprise tier live (€999/mo, team features)
- First US outreach (bigger budgets, same product)
- LinkedIn content: founder story + product demos
- Partner with 1 accountancy/legal firm for distribution

**Revenue:** €8000-12000/mo
**Burn:** €9k/mo
**Net:** roughly break-even or slight negative

---

### Month 11-12 — Series A prep
**Actions:**
- 15-20 B2B clients
- €15-20k MRR → Series A territory
- Prep Series A deck
- Target: Northzone, Atomico, Index Ventures (EU-focused)
- US accelerator application: Y Combinator (March/September batch)

**Revenue:** €15000-20000/mo
**Burn:** €9-12k/mo (team growing)
**Net:** €3-8k/mo positive
**Bank:** €80-120k remaining from pre-seed + revenue

---

## PHASE 4: SERIES A (Month 13-18)
*Goal: raise €1-3M. Dominate EU market.*

### Month 13-15 — Raise Series A
**Pitch:**
- €15-20k MRR growing 20%+ monthly
- 20+ B2B clients with testimonials
- Clear path to enterprise (compliance market = €10B+)
- Team of 3-5 people

**Ask:** €1-3M at €8-15M valuation
**Dilution:** 15-20% (you keep 70-75% after pre-seed + Series A)

**How to spend €2M:**
| Expense | Monthly | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Your salary | €5000/mo | Market rate founder salary |
| Dev team (3 people) | €12000/mo | Build enterprise features fast |
| Sales team (2 people) | €8000/mo | Close B2B deals at scale |
| Marketing | €5000/mo | Paid + content |
| Infrastructure | €2000/mo | Scale to enterprise |
| Legal/finance | €2000/mo | Enterprise contracts, compliance |
| **Total burn** | **€34k/mo** | |

**Runway:** €2M / €34k = ~58 months (nearly 5 years)
**Realistic:** you won't need all of it if revenue grows

---

### Month 16-18 — EU expansion
**Actions:**
- German market (biggest EU enterprise budget)
- UK market (post-Brexit still huge for B2B SaaS)
- Hire country manager or partner per market
- ISO 27001 certification (enterprise requirement)
- SOC 2 Type II (US enterprise requirement)

**Revenue:** €40-80k/mo
**Team:** 8-12 people
**Valuation:** €15-30M

**Revenue:** €150-300k/mo
**Team:** 20-30 people
**ARR:** €2-4M

### Month 31-36
**Two paths:**

**Path A — Keep building (Series B)**
- Raise €5-15M
- US HQ
- 50+ person team
- Target €10M ARR by month 48
- IPO or large acquisition in year 5-7

**Path B — Exit (acquisition)**
